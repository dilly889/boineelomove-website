More about us

We believe travel should change you

At BoineeloMove we believe that travel is more than just visiting unfamiliar places: it's creating a meaningful experience, with memories to back it up for a lifetime.

We're all about encouraging everyone to adventure more: breaking barriers and making travel exciting and stress-free with our bucket-list experiences. That's why we go above and beyond to carefully curate and customise itineraries for each one of our clients, solo trips, couples' getaways, family vacays or a friends' trip. Each trip is curated and executed with care and special diligence.

Our goal is to offer a unique, enriching travel experience that encourages personal growth and connection with the world, whether you travel as a group, solo, family or couple.

About our founder

It started with a note
after Thailand

I first started BoineeloMove in my head in 2017, on my notes after a trip to Thailand. My exposure to travel led to one of my friends asking me, "friend, why don't you make this travel a business?", and the rest is history.

I started with a ladies' trip to Cape Town in 2021, in the midst of COVID, with only five ladies after postponing the trip since 2019. The following year the ladies' trip sold out to 16. For me, that was the aha moment, I never could have imagined how quickly this would grow, and keeps growing.

I'm enthusiastic about travelling, and I believe travel has the power to broaden horizons, create lasting memories, and bring people together.
